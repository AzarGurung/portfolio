Hi, I’m Ajar… I build brands people remember.

I collect ventures like Pokémon cards and obsess over making each one tell a story worth sharing.

I’m a social media specialist and serial entrepreneur based in Kathmandu. For the last few years I’ve been doing one thing in a lot of different costumes… figuring out what makes people stop scrolling and building brands around it.

That’s taken me from filming property tours for a real estate agency, to running social for a restaurant brand in India, to founding my own social media marketing agency, Buzzing Bees. Along the way the work has pulled in 10M+ views, a 1.4M view reel, and a TikTok debut that crossed 100K.

These days I’m building three things at once: Buzzing Bees, Ajar Real Estate, and Project Offline, while teaching myself to use AI to automate my daily workflow. I think in systems, write it down, map it out, find the real problem, then weigh a few good options before committing.
